The Opportunity

$1.97B processed. 140M transactions. Infrastructure works flawlessly.

But consumer satisfaction is 3.1/5 while merchants love us at 4.2/5.

The gap isn't about our product working better‚Äîit's about consumer experience.

We need to make RedeemSG simpler, safer, and more accessible for the 3.1M+ consumers who use it.

Why Consumer Satisfaction Is Low

1. Link Hunting Friction
Every time user accesses vouchers: (1) Remember/find go.gov.sg/cdcv (2) Select campaign (3) Singpass login (4) Wait for SMS (5) Click link. No persistent dashboard.
Impact: Users abandon, forget links between uses
2. Phishing Vulnerability
Fake RedeemSG sites + SMS scams create trust anxiety. Users don't know if they're on the real site.
Impact: Increased churn, poor reviews
3. Discovery Problem
No clear way to find RedeemSG. Not in LifeSG. Not bookmarked by most users. Hunt + forget = friction.
Impact: Low repeat usage
4. Lack of Education
Users don't understand how to use RedeemSG safely. No in-app guidance on spotting fakes or claiming vouchers.
Impact: Preventable support inquiries

Phase 1: Eliminate Link Hunting (30 Days)

Current: 5-step journey (remember URL ‚Üí campaign selection ‚Üí Singpass ‚Üí SMS ‚Üí click)

Goal: 1 bookmark ‚Üí direct access to active vouchers

Persistent Voucher Dashboard (go.gov.sg/my-vouchers)

Create persistent "my vouchers" URL. After Singpass login once, redirects to user's active vouchers. No campaign selection step. Bookmark-friendly.

  • Effort: 3 days (backend redirect + session logic)
  • Impact: Eliminates 3 of 5 steps; users save & reuse URL

LifeSG Quick Access Widget

Add RedeemSG vouchers to LifeSG home screen. Users already have LifeSG installed + logged in. One-tap from familiar dashboard.

  • Effort: 1 week (API integration)
  • Impact: Leverages existing auth state; removes "remember URL" burden

Extend Session Duration

Change Singpass session timeout from 15 min to 30 days. User stays logged in. Eliminates daily re-authentication friction.

  • Effort: 1 day (config change)
  • Impact: Reduces "wait for SMS" step on repeat visits

Proactive SMS Reminders

Send SMS with voucher link 1 week before expiry. Include balance. User doesn't have to hunt for deleted link.

  • Effort: 3 days (SMS scheduler + template)
  • Impact: Prevents "where's my link?" support tickets
